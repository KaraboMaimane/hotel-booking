Who Are We??

Welcome to the first four-star luxury boutique hotel offering African hospitality in the heart of Soweto, a historically rich environment, with uniquely talented people focused on exceeding our guests’ expectations consistently! I am delighted to introduce you to a unique experience of boutique accommodation and conferencing, as can only be experienced at our establishment – graded with four stars by the Tourism Grading Council of South Africa. We are proud to offer you world-class facilities and amenities immersed with all the local flavour, culture, heritage and history of the most famous township – Kliptown, Soweto!
